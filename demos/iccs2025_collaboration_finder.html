Welcome to the ICCS 2025 Scientific Collaboration Finder

Start typing your name above to discover researchers who might be a good collaboration partners. Click ORCID links to view or search researcher profiles. Our mode analysis considers co-authorship network together with publication content to suggest top-10 meaningful connections with potential co-authored publications in the future. The suggestions are selected from the authors in ICCS 2025 so you can find their presentations accordingto the conference schedule. Note that the presentation can be given by the suggested researcher of his/her co-authors. For more details refer to our paper and poster: N. Dorzhu et al. Predicting future collaborations in a scientific community using graph neural networks / Proceedings of 25th International Conference on Computational Science (ICCS 2025), 2025.
